About Haribon Foundation

As the world progresses, nature more and more gets desecrated and neglected. We must not forget that nature is where we come from and where we end up at the end of our lives. We must learn to take care of the world we live in and do our best to preserve nature itself and take care of our environment so as to not worsen the state of the world we live in.The reason why I chose Haribon Foundation as a charity to support is mainly because:
1. They support the preservation and conservation of habitats for animals.
2. They take actions on saving species, especially endangered ones.
3. They inspire the community to provide more sustainable options in terms of environmental agendas and the likes.
